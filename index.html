About Me

I am a Software Quality Assurance Analyst with hands-on experience in manual and automated testing across web and application-based systems. My background includes functional, regression, system, integration, and API testing, with practical experience using tools such as Selenium for automation and Jira for defect tracking and delivery support. I focus on ensuring software reliability, usability, and high-quality user experiences throughout the development lifecycle. Beyond testing, I work closely with developers, project managers, and stakeholders in an agile startup environment, supporting sprint activities, user acceptance testing, and release readiness. I have also contributed to creating clear client briefs and coordinating sign-off for new features before development begins, helping reduce rework and scope-related risks. I have a solid technical foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, with working knowledge of Python, Java, and C#, which allows me to collaborate effectively with engineering teams and understand system behaviour beyond the surface level. While Quality Assurance remains my core strength, I am intentionally growing toward a hybrid role that combines QA with technical project coordination and delivery support.
