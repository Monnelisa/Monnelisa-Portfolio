Profile

Quality-first mindset with strong delivery support

I am a Software Quality Assurance Analyst with hands-on experience in manual and automated testing across web and application-based systems. My background includes functional, regression, system, integration, and API testing, with practical experience using Selenium for automation and Jira for defect tracking. I focus on ensuring software reliability, usability, and high-quality user experiences throughout the development lifecycle.

Beyond testing, I collaborate closely with developers, project managers, and stakeholders in an agile startup environment, supporting sprint activities, user acceptance testing, and release readiness. I have also contributed to creating clear client briefs and coordinating sign-off for new features before development begins, helping reduce rework and scope-related risks.

I have a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, with working knowledge of Python, Java, and C#. This allows me to collaborate effectively with engineering teams and understand system behavior beyond the surface level. While QA remains my core strength, I am growing toward a hybrid role that combines QA with technical project coordination and delivery support.
